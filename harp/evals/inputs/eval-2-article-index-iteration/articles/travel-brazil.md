Three weeks: a long stretch in São Paulo, then Rio, and a final week down the coast in Paraty. A few impressions, in no particular order.

São Paulo is denser and more European than I'd been led to expect. The food was excellent — pastel de feira on the way to the airport, two different kinds of açaí, and a feijoada at a place near Vila Madalena that I'm still thinking about.

Rio is exactly what the postcards say it is, except louder and warmer. We took the train up Corcovado at sunset and I'm not embarrassed to admit I got a little choked up.

Paraty I'd go back to tomorrow. Cobblestones, schooners, no traffic.
